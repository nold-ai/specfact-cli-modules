# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, collaborative, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and outcomes, not people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or intimidation.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to `hello@noldai.com`.
